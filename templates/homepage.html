Scanning your pictures for missing alt text

This is a software project created based on our final project for our computer science education. The project is solely made for helping companies look through their website and finding pictures without alt text, so they can comply with the European accessibility act

This software project, created by Kasper and Marcus from KEA, focuses solely on scanning websites for missing alt text on images. Alt text is crucial for accessibility, providing descriptions for images that assist users who may have visual impairments. Our software aims to streamline this process by identifying any instances where alt text is absent, ensuring websites are more inclusive and accessible for all users
